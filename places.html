Tourist Places

Siddheshwar Temple

Siddheshwar Temple is one of the most important spiritual landmarks in Solapur. Dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar, a form of Lord Shiva.

The temple is located near Siddheshwar Lake in the center of the city.

Thousands of devotees visit during the Siddheshwar Yatra festival in January.

Pandharpur

Pandharpur is one of the most sacred pilgrimage towns in Maharashtra located about 70 km from Solapur.

It is famous for the Vitthal Rukmini Temple dedicated to Lord Vitthal.

Akkalkot

Akkalkot is located about 40 km from Solapur and is famous for the temple of Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj.

Devotees believe Swami Samarth blesses people and helps them overcome difficulties.

Solapur Fort

Solapur Fort, also called Bhuikot Fort, was built during the Bahmani Sultanate period.

The fort reflects the military architecture of medieval India.
